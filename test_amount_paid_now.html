💰 Amount Paid Now - Advance Payment Demo

🎯 Updated Functionality:

Amount Paid Now is stored in customer account balance (NOT deducted from current sale).

The Total shows the full sale amount (what they pay now).

📋 Example Scenario:

Cart Total: 200.00 DH

Amount Paid Now: 50.00 DH (stored for customer)

Customer Pays Now: 200.00 DH

Customer Balance Update: +50.00 DH (added to their account)

🛒 How It Works:

  • Customer selects "Customer" type
  • "Amount Paid Now" field appears
  • Enter amount customer is paying today (stored in their account)
  • Total shows full sale amount (NOT reduced by advance)
  • Customer's account balance is updated separately
  • Next visit shows their updated balance
