🔧 How It Works:

  • View Customer: Go to customers/view.php?id=X
  • Transaction History: Shows all customer transactions with details
  • Pay Rest Button: Click to pay remaining amount for any transaction
  • Real-time Updates: Transactions update immediately after payment
  • Summary Stats: Shows totals across all transactions

🗄️ Database Tables Created:
